#6 Future Predictions

← Back to Projects

For this project, I made some predictions about what the future might look like across several major themes. Here are some of my predictions:

Fashion

Clothes will be the same as always but will get lessexpensive because the materials to make them will get cheaper.

Health and Medicine

Medicine will become incredibly personalized thanks to AI. AI is already and will revolutionize the health and medical world.

Housing

Homes will be fully integrated, self-sustaining smart environments. They will generate their own renewable energy, manage their own waste, and use AI to anticipate our needs—like adjusting lighting, ordering groceries, or regulating the climate automatically before we even walk through the door.

Politics

Voting and civic participation will likely become entirely digital, secured by blockchain technology to make it accessible and fraud-proof.

Shopping

Physical stores will become interactive showrooms. You will try on clothes or test items using Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality from your living room, and a drone will deliver the actual product to your house within minutes.

Social Media

Social Media will be the same as ever with just some more ai slop.
