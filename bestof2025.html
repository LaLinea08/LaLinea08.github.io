#4 Best of 2025

← Back to Projects

For this project, I decided to explore some new music. I listened to 1 song from 5 different albums released (or discovered) in 2025 and rated them based on production, lyrics, and overall vibe. Here are my thoughts:


1. "Neon Horizons" — from the album Midnight Echoes

Rating: 8.5 / 10
This track has an amazing retro-synth feel but sounds very modern. The beat drops exactly when it needs to, and the chorus gets stuck in your head almost immediately. A great song for late-night drives.

2. "Acoustic Tides" — from the album Ocean Whispers

Rating: 6.0 / 10
I usually like indie-folk, but this one felt a bit too slow for my taste. The guitar work is beautiful and the lyrics are deep, but it lacks the energy I usually look for in a daily playlist.

3. "Urban Symphony" — from the album City Lights

Rating: 9.0 / 10
The production on this hip-hop track is insane. The bass is heavy, the flow is smooth, and the background samples create a really cool atmosphere. Definitely adding this to my workout playlist.

4. "Fading Shadows" — from the album The Rock Revival

Rating: 7.5 / 10
A solid alternative rock song. It starts off slow and builds up to a massive guitar solo at the end. It takes a minute to get going, but the payoff at the end is worth it.

5. "Galactic Groove" — from the album Future Pop 2025

Rating: 10 / 10
Absolute perfection. It’s catchy, upbeat, and impossible not to dance to. Everything from the vocals to the electronic beat works perfectly together. easily my favorite track of the bunch.


Conclusion: Stepping outside my usual playlists was a fun experiment. I definitely found a few new favorites to keep on repeat!
