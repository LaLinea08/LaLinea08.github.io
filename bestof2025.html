#4 Best of 2025

← Back to Projects

For this project, I decided to explore some new music. I listened to 1 song from 5 different albums released in 2025 and rated them.
1. "Neon Horizons" from the album Midnight Echoes

Rating: 8.5 / 10
This track has an amazing retro style, but sounds very modern.

2. "Acoustic Tides" from the album Ocean Whispers

Rating: 6.0 / 10
I usually like indie-folk, but this one felt a bit too slow for my taste.

3. "Urban Symphony" from the album City Lights

Rating: 9.0 / 10
Definitely adding this to my workout playlist.

4. "Fading Shadows" from the album The Rock Revival

Rating: 7.5 / 10
It starts off slow and builds up to a massive guitar solo at the end. It takes a minute to get going

5. "Galactic Groove" from the album Future Pop 2025

Rating: 10 / 10
It’s catchy, upbeat. Easily my favorite track of the bunch.


Conclusion: Stepping outside my usual playlists was a fun experiment. I definitely found a few new favorites to keep on repeat!
